## Introduction

Halo Safety Intelligence ("Halo", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and web platform (collectively, the "Service").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not use the Service.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide

**Account Information:**
- Email address (if you create an account)
- Password (encrypted)
- Display name (optional)

**Incident Reports:**
- Incident description
- Location data (GPS coordinates)
- Date and time
- Photos, videos, or audio recordings (optional)
- Incident type and severity

**Note:** Incident reports can be submitted anonymously without creating an account.

### 2. Automatically Collected Information

**Location Data:**
- Real-time GPS coordinates (when app is in use)
- Location history (for geofence alerts only)
- IP address-based location (web version)

**Device Information:**
- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- App version
- Unique device identifiers
- Mobile network information

**Usage Data:**
- Features you use
- Screens you view
- Time spent in app
- Crash reports and error logs
- Performance data

### 3. Information from Third Parties

**Public Crime Data:**
- We collect publicly available crime data from Polisen.se (Swedish Police)
- This data is anonymized and aggregated

## Data Retention

**Location Data:**
- Real-time location is NOT stored on our servers
- Location is only used temporarily for geofence alerts
- Geofence location history: Stored locally on device only

**Incident Reports:**
- Stored indefinitely (anonymized after 90 days)
- Can be deleted upon request

**Account Data:**
- Stored until you delete your account
- Deleted within 30 days of account deletion request

**Logs and Analytics:**
- Stored for 90 days
- Automatically deleted after retention period

## Location Data and Permissions

### Location Permission Types

**"When In Use":**
- App can access location only when you're using it
- Used for map display and incident reporting

**"Always":**
- App can access location in the background
- Required for geofence safety alerts
- You can change this in device Settings at any time

### How We Use Location

- **Map Display:** Show your current location on the map
- **Incident Reporting:** Automatically fill location when submitting reports
- **Geofence Alerts:** Notify you when entering high-risk areas
- **Predictions:** Display crime predictions for your area

### Location Storage

- Location data is NOT stored on our servers
- Used only temporarily for calculations
- Geofence coordinates stored locally on your device

You can revoke location permission at any time in device Settings.
