Privacy Policy

Summary: Halo collects location data and incident reports to provide safety predictions. We never sell your data. You can report incidents anonymously. You have full control over your data under GDPR.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Halo Safety Intelligence ("Halo," "we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application and services.

Halo is committed to protecting your privacy and complying with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable data protection laws.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Location Data

We collect your device's location data to provide core safety features:

  • Foreground Location: When you open the app to view nearby incidents and safety predictions
  • Background Location: Only when you enable geofence alerts, to notify you when entering high-risk areas
  • Incident Location: When you report an incident, we collect the location where it occurred

Important: Location data is processed locally on your device for map display. We only transmit coordinates (latitude/longitude) to our servers when you submit an incident report or request safety predictions for a specific area.

2.2 Incident Reports

When you report a disturbance or incident, we collect:

  • Incident type (e.g., disturbance, suspicious activity)
  • Location coordinates (latitude/longitude)
  • Date and time of incident
  • Optional description text
  • Optional media files (photos, videos, audio recordings)
  • Device information (device type, OS version) for quality assurance

Anonymous Reporting: You can submit incident reports without creating an account. Anonymous reports are not linked to any personal identifier.

2.3 Account Information (Optional)

If you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address (for login and notifications)
  • Password (encrypted and stored securely)
  • Account creation date
  • Notification preferences

2.4 Device and Usage Data

We automatically collect technical information:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version and build number
  • Crash reports and error logs (via Sentry)
  • App usage statistics (feature usage, screen views)
  • Network connection type (WiFi, cellular)

Purpose: This data helps us fix bugs, improve performance, and enhance user experience.

2.5 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT track your location continuously in the background (only when you enable geofence alerts)
  • We do NOT collect contact lists, SMS messages, or phone call data
  • We do NOT use third-party advertising trackers
  • We do NOT sell your personal data to anyone

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Primary Purposes

We use your data to provide and improve Halo's core features:

  • Safety Predictions: Generate AI-powered crime risk predictions by analyzing patterns in incident data
  • Incident Mapping: Display reported incidents on an interactive map
  • Geofence Alerts: Send you notifications when you enter high-risk areas (only if enabled)
  • Community Intelligence: Combine official police data with community reports for better predictions
  • App Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to fix bugs and add features

3.2 Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your personal data based on:

  • Consent: Location tracking, geofence alerts, and push notifications (you can withdraw consent anytime)
  • Legitimate Interest: Safety predictions and incident mapping serve our legitimate interest in public safety
  • Contract Performance: Processing necessary to provide the service you requested
  • Legal Obligation: Complying with law enforcement requests when legally required

3.3 Automated Decision-Making

Halo uses machine learning algorithms to generate safety predictions. These predictions are based on historical crime patterns and are intended as informational guidance only. No automated decisions are made that produce legal effects or significantly affect you.

4. How We Share Your Information

4.1 We Do NOT Sell Your Data

Halo will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4.2 Data Sharing with Service Providers

We share limited data with trusted service providers who help us operate Halo:

  • Scaleway (France): Cloud hosting for backend servers and database (GDPR-compliant, EU-based)
  • Sentry (USA): Crash reporting and error monitoring (Privacy Shield certified)
  • Nominatim/OpenStreetMap: Geocoding service to convert addresses to coordinates (open-source, privacy-respecting)

Data Processing Agreements: All service providers are contractually required to protect your data and use it only for specified purposes.

4.3 Public Incident Data

Incident reports you submit (location, type, time) may be displayed anonymously on the public map. We do NOT reveal reporter identity, email addresses, or account information.

4.4 Law Enforcement Requests

We may disclose information to law enforcement if legally required by Swedish law or valid court order. We will notify you of such requests unless prohibited by law.

4.5 Anonymized Research Data

We may share anonymized, aggregated statistics with academic researchers studying crime prevention. This data cannot be used to identify any individual.

5. Data Retention

5.1 How Long We Keep Your Data

  • Incident Reports: Stored indefinitely in anonymized form for historical crime analysis
  • Location Logs: Not stored long-term; only used for immediate safety predictions
  • Account Data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Crash Reports: Retained for 90 days for debugging purposes
  • App Usage Analytics: Aggregated and anonymized after 30 days

5.2 Deletion of Old Data

We automatically delete or anonymize personal identifiers after the retention period. Incident data is anonymized (reporter ID removed) after 30 days.

6. Your Rights Under GDPR

As a user in the European Union, you have the following rights:

6.1 Right to Access

You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you. Email tim@archeron.tech with subject "Data Access Request."

6.2 Right to Rectification

You can correct inaccurate personal information by updating your account settings or contacting us.

6.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")

You can request deletion of your account and personal data. Note: Anonymized incident reports will remain for public safety purposes.

6.4 Right to Data Portability

You can export your data in machine-readable format (JSON). Email tim@archeron.tech with subject "Data Export Request."

6.5 Right to Restrict Processing

You can request that we limit how we use your data, for example by disabling geofence alerts.

6.6 Right to Object

You can object to data processing based on legitimate interest. We will stop unless we have compelling legitimate grounds.

6.7 Right to Withdraw Consent

You can withdraw consent for location tracking or push notifications anytime in app settings.

6.8 Right to Lodge a Complaint

If you believe we've violated your privacy rights, you can file a complaint with the Swedish Data Protection Authority (Datainspektionen): www.imy.se

7. Data Security

7.1 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses TLS/SSL encryption
  • Encryption at Rest: Database is encrypted using AES-256 encryption
  • Secure Authentication: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt (never stored in plain text)
  • Access Control: Only authorized personnel can access production systems
  • Regular Backups: Daily encrypted backups stored securely
  • Security Monitoring: 24/7 monitoring for suspicious activity

7.2 Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify you and relevant authorities within 72 hours as required by GDPR.

8. Children's Privacy

Halo is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact tim@archeron.tech and we will delete it promptly.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data is primarily stored in the European Union (Scaleway servers in France). Some service providers (e.g., Sentry) may process data in the United States under appropriate safeguards (Privacy Shield, Standard Contractual Clauses).

  • Updated "Last Modified" date at the top of this page

Continued use of Halo after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
