2. Description of Service

2.1 What Halo Does

Halo is a public safety intelligence platform that:

  • Displays AI-powered crime risk predictions for Swedish cities
  • Shows reported incidents on an interactive map
  • Allows users to report disturbances and suspicious activity
  • Sends optional geofence alerts when entering high-risk areas
  • Combines official police data (polisen.se) with community reports

2.2 Data Sources

Halo uses the following data sources:

  • Official Police Data: Public crime reports from Swedish Police (polisen.se)
  • Community Reports: User-submitted incident reports (verified and anonymized)
  • Machine Learning Models: AI algorithms that analyze historical patterns
